Junior Entreprise ENISO

A junior enterprise is a civil social nonprofit organization established in 2011 and executed entirely by students of a university or a business school, both at the undergraduate and postgraduate level. The purpose of the organization is to provide services for companies, institutions and society, often in the form of consultancies, while enriching the learning of its members with practical experience in the field of their studies. .
